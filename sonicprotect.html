One Subscription. Everything You Need.

SonicProtect is SonicWall's all-inclusive subscription that bundles firewall hardware, security services, support, and management into a single predictable payment. Simplify procurement and ensure continuous protection. No surprise renewal costs, no lapsed coverage, no piecemeal licensing. One SKU covers your firewall, every security service, 24/7 support, and cloud management through Network Security Manager.

What's Included

Every SonicProtect subscription bundles these essential components

Next-Gen Firewall Hardware

Your choice of SonicWall TZ, NSa, or NSsp series firewall appliance included in the subscription. Hardware is replaced at no additional cost during the subscription term.

Essential Protection Service Suite

Gateway anti-virus, anti-spyware, intrusion prevention, and content filtering. All the core threat prevention services active from day one.

24/7 Technical Support

Around-the-clock access to SonicWall support engineers via phone, email, and web portal. Includes hardware replacement with advance exchange.

Network Security Manager

Cloud-based centralized management included at no additional cost. Monitor, configure, and manage your firewall from anywhere through NSM SaaS.

Firmware Updates & Upgrades

Continuous firmware updates, security patches, and feature upgrades for the lifetime of your subscription. Always running the latest protection.

$1M Cyber Warranty

Qualifying SonicProtect subscriptions include breach cost coverage up to $1 million. Real financial commitment backing real protection.
