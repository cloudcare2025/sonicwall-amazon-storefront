Key Capabilities

Enterprise SD-WAN features built into every SonicWall Gen 7 firewall

Zero-Touch Deployment

Ship firewalls to remote sites and provision them automatically through SonicWall's cloud management platform. No on-site IT required.

Application-Aware Routing

Identify and route application traffic by type. Prioritize business-critical apps like VoIP and video while offloading general traffic to broadband.

Multi-WAN Failover

Automatic failover across broadband, LTE, and MPLS connections. Sub-second switchover ensures continuous uptime for critical operations.

Centralized Management

Manage all SD-WAN policies, monitoring, and analytics from a single pane of glass through Network Security Manager (NSM).

Use Cases

How organizations deploy SonicWall Secure SD-WAN to solve real connectivity challenges
