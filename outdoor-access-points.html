Where It Deploys

Purpose-built for environments where indoor access points cannot survive.

Campus Grounds

Extend secure Wi-Fi coverage to outdoor campus areas, courtyards, and walkways. Maintain the same security policies and management from indoors to outdoors with zero coverage gaps.

Warehouses & Yards

Weather-resistant connectivity for industrial staging areas, loading docks, and shipping yards. IP67 protection means reliable operation in rain, dust, and extreme temperatures.

Hospitality

Deliver guest Wi-Fi to pool areas, patios, rooftop terraces, and outdoor dining spaces. Band steering and airtime fairness keep every guest connected.
