The Premium Tier of Managed Security

SonicSentry MXDR is the highest level of managed security operations from SonicWall. It combines 24/7 SOC coverage with proactive threat hunting, automated response, and cross-platform correlation across your entire infrastructure.

Unlike traditional MDR that focuses on a single domain, MXDR extends visibility and response across endpoints, network traffic, cloud workloads, and email — giving your security team a unified defense posture managed by dedicated analysts.

How MXDR Differs from MDR

  • Broader coverage across endpoints, network, cloud, and email
  • Proactive threat hunting, not just monitoring and alerting
  • Automated response and remediation workflows
  • Dedicated analyst assigned to your environment
  • Cross-stack correlation for advanced attack chain detection

Key MXDR Capabilities

Enterprise-grade security operations delivered by expert analysts around the clock

24/7 SOC Coverage

Around-the-clock security operations center staffed by certified analysts who monitor, triage, and respond to threats in real time across every domain.
