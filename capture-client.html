Endpoint Security That Integrates With Your Firewall

Capture Client delivers next-generation endpoint protection using SentinelOne's autonomous AI engine. It prevents, detects, and responds to threats across Windows, macOS, and Linux — all managed from a single cloud console.

Unlike standalone endpoint products, Capture Client integrates directly with SonicWall firewalls to enforce VPN connectivity, verify endpoint health before granting network access, and share threat intelligence across the security stack.

Firewall Integration

  • Enforce always-on VPN connectivity through the firewall
  • Verify endpoint health status before network access
  • Share threat intelligence between endpoint and firewall
  • Centralized policy management across both platforms
