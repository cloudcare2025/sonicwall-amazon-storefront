Network-focused managed detection and response. Expert analysts monitoring your SonicWall firewall telemetry for threats around the clock.

Explore Network MDR → Contact Sales

Firewall-Powered Managed Detection & Response

SonicSentry MDR for Network leverages the rich telemetry from your SonicWall firewalls to provide expert-managed threat detection and response. Every connection, every flow, every protocol anomaly is analyzed by certified security analysts.

By correlating firewall logs, IPS/IDS alerts, and network traffic patterns, the MDR team detects lateral movement, command-and-control callbacks, data exfiltration attempts, and advanced threats that evade signature-based detection alone.

SonicWall Firewall Integration

  • Native integration with TZ, NSa, and NSsp series firewalls
  • Real-time log ingestion via syslog and API
  • IPS/IDS alert correlation and enrichment
  • VPN and SSL/TLS traffic inspection telemetry
  • Automated policy adjustment recommendations
