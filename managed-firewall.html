What Is the Managed Protection Security Suite?

SonicWall's Managed Protection Security Suite (MPSS) combines hardware, security services, and expert management into a single, comprehensive offering. Instead of managing your firewall infrastructure in-house, you get a dedicated security team that handles it all.

Your SonicWall experts take ownership of firmware updates, policy management, threat response, and ongoing optimization — so your IT team can focus on strategic initiatives instead of day-to-day firewall operations.
