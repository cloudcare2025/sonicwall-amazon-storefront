Single-Pane-of-Glass Management

Cloud-native wireless management built for distributed networks

Wireless Network Manager (WNM) is SonicWall's cloud-based management platform for centralized visibility and control of SonicWave access points and SonicWall switches. Manage your entire wireless infrastructure across distributed locations from a single console, eliminating the complexity of site-by-site configuration and monitoring.

Key Capabilities

Everything you need to deploy, manage, and optimize your wireless network

Centralized Dashboard

Monitor all access points and switches across every location from one cloud console. Real-time health, status, and performance at a glance.

Zero-Touch Deployment

Access points auto-register and pull their configuration when powered on. No on-site IT required. Ship, plug in, done.

WiFi Planner

RF planning tool to optimize AP placement before deployment. Upload floor plans, simulate coverage, and eliminate dead zones before installing a single device.

Guest Portal

Customizable captive portal for guest wireless access. Brand your login page, set bandwidth limits, enforce acceptable use, and track guest activity.

Rich Analytics

Real-time and historical data on client connections, traffic patterns, and network performance. Identify issues before users notice them.

Firmware Management

Schedule and push firmware updates across all devices from the cloud. Maintain security compliance without touching a single device on site.

How It Works

From unboxing to full management in three steps

1

Connect

Plug in SonicWave access points or SonicWall switches at any location. Power on and connect to the network.

2

Configure

Devices auto-register in the WNM cloud dashboard. Pre-staged policies and configurations apply automatically.

3

Manage

Monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize your entire wireless infrastructure from anywhere with a browser.
