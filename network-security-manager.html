Centralized Management

NETWORK SECURITY MANAGER

Cloud-native centralized management for your entire SonicWall firewall fleet. Single-pane visibility, policy orchestration, and real-time analytics.

Explore NSM

One Dashboard. Total Control.

Network Security Manager is SonicWall's cloud-based management platform for centralized firewall governance. Deploy, manage, and monitor hundreds of firewalls from a single dashboard. Whether you operate a single office or a distributed enterprise with thousands of endpoints, NSM gives you unified visibility and control over every SonicWall appliance in your fleet.

Key Features

Everything you need to manage your firewall fleet at scale

Centralized Policy Management

Create, push, and enforce unified security policies across every firewall in your fleet from a single console. Eliminate configuration drift and ensure consistent protection.

Real-Time Monitoring & Alerts

Live dashboards with threat intelligence, bandwidth analytics, and instant alerting. Know exactly what is happening across every location in real time.

Automated Firmware Updates

Schedule and deploy firmware updates across your entire fleet with zero-touch automation. Maintain compliance without on-site visits or manual intervention.

Compliance & Audit Reporting

Generate detailed audit trails and compliance reports with a single click. Track every policy change, user action, and security event for regulatory requirements.

Multi-Tenant Support

Manage multiple customers or business units from a single NSM instance with full tenant isolation. Ideal for MSSPs, MSPs, and large distributed enterprises.

Template-Based Deployment

Define security policy templates once and deploy them to new firewalls instantly. Accelerate site deployments from days to minutes with consistent configurations.

How It Works

Three steps from deployment to unified firewall management

1

Connect Firewalls

Register your SonicWall firewalls with NSM using zero-touch provisioning. New and existing appliances connect automatically via serial number or token.

2

Define Policies

Create security policies, access rules, and firewall configurations from centralized templates. Push consistent policies to one device or your entire fleet.

3

Monitor & Optimize

Track real-time threat intelligence, bandwidth usage, and device health across every location. Receive instant alerts and drill into granular analytics.
