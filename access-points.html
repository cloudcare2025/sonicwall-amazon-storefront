Built for Performance, Security, and Simplicity

Every SonicWave access point is engineered around three core pillars.

Performance

802.11ax with OFDMA and MU-MIMO delivers maximum throughput and efficiency. Up to 8x8 spatial streams and 5GbE uplink eliminate bottlenecks even in the most demanding environments.

Security

WPA3 encryption, Capture ATP cloud sandbox, and Real-Time Deep Memory Inspection (RTDMI) work together to detect and block advanced threats at the wireless edge.

Management

Wireless Network Manager cloud dashboard, WiFi Planner tool, and Zero-Touch Deployment simplify provisioning, monitoring, and management across every site.

Technology Highlights

Enterprise-grade wireless technologies that keep your network fast, secure, and manageable.

Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax)

OFDMA, BSS Coloring, and Target Wake Time deliver higher efficiency, lower latency, and better battery life for connected devices.

WPA3 Security

The latest encryption standard with Simultaneous Authentication of Equals (SAE) provides enhanced protection against offline dictionary attacks.

Capture ATP

Cloud-based multi-engine sandbox analyzes suspicious files and detects advanced threats, including zero-day exploits and ransomware.

Zero-Touch Deployment

Automatic provisioning and configuration through the cloud. Connect, power on, and access points self-configure with your saved policies.
