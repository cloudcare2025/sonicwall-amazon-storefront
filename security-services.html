Subscription FAQ

Common questions about SonicWall security service subscriptions

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your security service subscription expires, your SonicWall firewall continues to function as a stateful packet inspection firewall. However, all advanced security services -- including gateway anti-virus, intrusion prevention, content filtering, and Capture ATP sandboxing -- will stop receiving updates and cease active protection. Your network will no longer be protected against new threats. We strongly recommend renewing before expiration to maintain continuous protection.

Can I upgrade my bundle mid-term?

Yes. You can upgrade from Essential to Advanced or from Advanced to Total Security at any time during your subscription term. The upgrade cost is prorated based on the remaining time on your current subscription. Contact SonicWall support or your authorized reseller to process the upgrade. The new services activate immediately upon upgrade.

Do I need a separate subscription for each firewall?

Yes. Security service subscriptions are tied to individual firewall appliances by serial number. Each SonicWall firewall in your network requires its own subscription. However, volume pricing is available for organizations deploying multiple units. Centralized management through SonicWall Network Security Manager (NSM) simplifies subscription tracking across your entire fleet.

What's included in 24/7 Support?

SonicWall 24/7 Support includes around-the-clock access to technical support engineers, firmware updates and security patches, hardware replacement with advance exchange, access to the SonicWall knowledge base and community forums, and configuration assistance. Support is available via phone, email, and web portal. Average response time is under 2 hours for critical issues.
