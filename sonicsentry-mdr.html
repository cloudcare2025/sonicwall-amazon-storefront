Expert Security Operations, Delivered as a Service

SonicSentry MDR provides 24/7 threat monitoring, incident investigation, and guided response from SonicWall's team of certified security analysts. It is designed for organizations that need SOC-level protection without the cost and complexity of building one internally.

From alert triage to full incident investigation, the MDR team works as an extension of your IT department — filtering noise, escalating real threats, and providing clear guidance on how to respond.
